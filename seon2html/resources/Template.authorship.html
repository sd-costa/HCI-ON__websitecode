Networked Ontologies development & assessment infomartion.

development

All networked ontologies were developed by following SABiO (FALBO, 2014), which prescribes that the ontology scope must be defined by means of competency questions, i.e., questions the ontology must be able to answer and are used as a basis to develop the ontology conceptual model.

assessment

To evaluate networked ontologies, we performed Ontology Verification & Validation (V&V) activities. Considering the guidelines proposed by SABiO (FALBO, 2014), networked ontologies were evaluated by using two evaluation approaches: assessment by human approach and data-driven approach (BRANK; GROBELNIK; MLADENIC ́ , 2005).
